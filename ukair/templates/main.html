Notes

Non-ATZ. Non-ATZ airfields are those listed as training aerodromes in section ENR 5.5 of the AIP. Note - many of the smaller airfields shown on the 1:500000 chart are not listed in the AIP and so are not included by this program.

Ultralight Strips. Microlight strips are also listed in ENR 5.5

Obstacles. Only obstacles (mainly radio masts) with a height of greater than 600' are included.
