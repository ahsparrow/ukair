Output Format

The recommended output format is OpenAir. Tim Newport-Peace (TNP) format should be used with caution; it is not interpreted consistently by all software.

Airspace

No-ATZ. No-ATZ airfields are those listed as training aerodromes in section ENR 5.5 of the AIP. Note - many of the smaller airfields shown on the 1:500000 chart are not listed in the AIP and are not included by this program.

Gliding Site. Gliding sites are shown with a radius of 1nm.

Microlight Strip. Microlight strips as listed in ENR 5.5.

Obstacle. Only obstacles (mainly radio masts) with a height of greater than 600' are included.

ATZ. ATZ's are controlled airspace. The BGA recommend setting them to class D.

ILS Feather. Although not controlled airspace the BGA recommend a radio call if you are in the vicinity and setting them to class D as a reminder.

Letters of Agreement (LoA)

Letters of Agreement are listed on the BGA website. Local conditions apply to their use.
